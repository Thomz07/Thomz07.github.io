UTrynaShutDown

UTrynaShutDown is a tweak that prevents people from shutting down your phone with a funny message

It is compatible for iOS 5 up to iOS 13

This tweak is not meant to secure your device, it is just to "prank" with your friends when they want to annoy you

Get it for free on Packix repository

This tweak is Open Source
